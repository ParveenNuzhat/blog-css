Yummy Food

I’m the type of indecisive person who can never pick favorites when it comes to most things in life. But somehow when it comes to food, where I would assume it’s hardest for me to pick favorites given how much I savor eating everything, there is one dish that for me has always ruled supreme: homestyle Bangladeshi chicken korma. Of all my cravings, this is the most frequent. Of all my blissful childhood memories of food, this is the fondest.
